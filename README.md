## Info:

*What this site is*: A resource for cartographers to quickly find inspiration among peers.

*What this site is not*: An endorsement of any individual or their work, nor intended to generate clients or sales (though some store pages may be linked directly where an individual has no equivalent portfolio).

Want to add/edit/remove? Reach out to koelker12 @ the email service provided by Google

## Background:

For many years now, I've been maintaining a bookmark list of portfolios showcasing maps from working cartographers and artists. The purpose was (is) to try and keep up with what fellow mapmakers are doing out there and occasionally get a quick jolt of inspiration. Lately I thought others might find this list helpful as well. I've also been meaning to try out GitHub Pages; so I made this page to try and knock out two birds with one stone.
Full disclosure, I am not a web developer, and I relied *heavily* on CoPilot to help create this page, tweaking things as needed. It's been a helpful learning excercise for both AI (both strengths and limitations) and HTML/JS but I'm sure there's still lots of room for improvement, so open to helpful suggestions. The goal of the page is to deliver cartographic goodness quickly and easily via portfolio sites. Think StumbleUpon (if you remember that site) but for active mapmakers and map-adjacent designers.
